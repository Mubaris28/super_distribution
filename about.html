Our Story

We connect households and businesses across Mauritius with trustworthy brands in several categories such as Home Care, Personal Care, Religious Products, Stationery and Industrial Packaging.

Who we are

Super Distribution Ltd. is Mauritius's trusted distributor for household essentials, eco-friendly tableware, personal care, and religious products. We work with retailers, wholesalers, and businesses across the island to keep shelves stocked with quality goods.

What we do

At Super Distribution Ltd, we combine quality, affordability, and sustainability. We source premium raw materials and convert them into dependable products while maintaining environmentally responsible practices, including eco-friendly solutions like Super Bio compostable tableware.

Beyond manufacturing, we distribute well-known brands such as Ujala, Margo, and Maya incense, ensuring consistent supply and reliable delivery. With fast dispatch and flexible order quantities, we make it easy for retailers and wholesalers to keep their shelves stocked and their customers satisfied.

Mission

To build lasting trust with our customers by providing high-quality, reliable, and innovative products and services at competitive prices, while ensuring efficient distribution and exceptional service.

Vision

To become a trusted leader in manufacturing and distribution by delivering sustainable products, supporting our partners, and continuously improving to meet the changing needs of our customers and communities.

Sustainability Goals

We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint and promoting responsible consumption. Our sustainability goals include expanding our range of compostable and eco-friendly products, such as Super Bio tableware, and working with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainable practices.

We aim to minimise waste in our operations, use recyclable or biodegradable packaging where possible, and support the transition to a circular economy. By offering quality products that are better for the planet, we help our retail and wholesale partners meet the growing demand for sustainable options across Mauritius.

Eco Friendly Products

Providing eco friendly products to the market. We are committed to reducing environmental impact through sustainable materials, compostable packaging, and responsible sourcing so that quality and affordability go hand in hand with care for the planet.

Community & Social Impact

We engage in social activities to foster connection and the well-being of needy people in our community. Giving back is part of who we are.
